Our story

Craft first, always in person.

Glamorous Nails started as one chair at Haatso Supermarket Junction and grew on repeat visits — not ads.

Since day one

Built around the chair, not the marketing

Every set starts with a short consultation: shape, length, and shade, matched to what your hands actually do all week. We keep a tight, transparent menu instead of ever-changing packages, so you always know what you're paying for before you sit down.

Tools are sterilized between every client, and appointments run on real time slots — no overbooking, no rushed finishes.
